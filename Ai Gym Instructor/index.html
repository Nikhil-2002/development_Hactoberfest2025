Quick Fitness Tips

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day, more if you're active.
  • Warm-Up Always: Spend 5-10 minutes on light cardio to prevent injuries.
  • Rest & Recover: Muscles grow during rest—aim for 7-9 hours of sleep.
  • Track Progress: Log your workouts to stay motivated and adjust plans.
  • Nutrition Matters: Fuel with balanced meals: proteins, carbs, and healthy fats.
